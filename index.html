Welcome to the Potato Kingdom

Everything you ever wanted to know about spuds

1. The Mighty Russet

Russet potatoes are the kings of baking and frying. They have a rough, brown skin and a fluffy texture when cooked, making them absolutely perfect for making crispy french fries or giant loaded baked potatoes.

2. The Smooth Yukon Gold

Yukon Golds have a yellowish skin and a naturally buttery flavor. Because they are incredibly smooth and creamy, chefs love to use them for making the ultimate mashed potatoes.

Potato Fun Fact!

Did you know? Potatoes were the very first vegetable to ever be grown in space! NASA and the University of Wisconsin-Madison tested potato plants aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia way back in 1995.
